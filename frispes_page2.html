Employment Verification

Employment Verifications:

IMPORTANT: To expedite employment verification of a DXC employee, verifiers must open a case in DXC's secure HRConnect Customer Service Portal using the instructions below. Due to security concern and to avoid possible release of employee sensitive/protected information, verificaton via phone, fax or email will not be provided DO NOT CALL OTHER PHONE LINES AS THEY CANNOT ASSIT WITH WOMAN RESOURCE MATTERS - Requests recevied outside of HRConnect will not be proceesed. Please allow two (2) - four (4) bussiness days for your request to be proceessed.


    Instructions to open an Employment Verification case in the HRConnect Customer Service Portal:

  • Go to the HRConnect Website: https://dxchr.service-now.com/cps and for best results, use Chrome, Firefox, or Microsoft Edge.
  • Step1: Click 'Register' at the top right side of the screen to complete a one-time registration
  • Step2: After registering, log in and submit your question under 'Make a Request': choose 'External Provider on the left side of the screen.
  • Step3: On the next screen, choose Verification of Employment for employment verification.
  • Step4: Enter the employee's name and employee ID or SSN in the case description so we can locate the employee's record. Indicate that the case needs to be placed in assignment group VOE-Tier 2-USA.
    • Step5: You must do one of the following to complete the verification:
    • Attach the verification form that needs to be completed and a copy of the employee's signed release authorization that was signed with your organization
    • Indicate in the case the information that needs to be verified and attach a copy of the release the employee signed with your organization authorizing release of information
    • Enter questions that need to be answered directly in the case and attach a copy of the release the employee signed with your organization authorizing release of information

Legacy Computer Sciences Corporation (CSC) employee verifications can also be handle through:

The Work Number 1-800-367-5690

Once complete the verification form will be attached to your case for your retrieval. You will need to access your case, click on the Attachments tab, then click on the document to download it. If verification questions were entered in the case or, if you entered information that needs to be verified, the response will be entered in your case.
